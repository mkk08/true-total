# Privacy Policy

True Total does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

The extension allows users to enter a ZIP code or manual tax rate to estimate sales tax when displaying total prices. This information is stored locally on the user’s device and is never sent to external servers.

True Total does not collect names, email addresses, payment information, browsing history, or user activity.

All calculations occur locally within the browser.

If you have questions, you may contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
